About Us

Zach and Avery Hutzell are the husband-and-wife team behind Hutzell Creative Co. Zach is a graduate student studying horticulture, and Avery is a full-time graphic designer for a local nonprofit. In every spare moment they have, they love creating together. Nothing excites them more than walking alongside people as they celebrate life’s biggest moments — from starting a business to starting a life together in marriage.
